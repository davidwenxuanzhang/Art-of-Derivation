# Integral with $x^2 \pm a^2$

### 19
$$
\int\frac{1}{x^2+a^2}\ \mathrm{d}x=\frac{1}{a}\arctan\left(\frac{x}{a}\right)+C
$$
<br/>
<details>
<summary><span className="math-keyword">Derivation</span></summary>

$$
\begin{align*}
    I&=\int{\frac{1}{x^2+a^2}}\ \mathrm{d}x\\
     &=\int{\frac{1}{a^2\left(\frac{x^2}{a^2}+1\right)}}\ \frac{\mathrm{d}\left(\frac{x}{a}\right)}{\frac{1}{a}} &&\text{(Direct Substitution)}\\
     &=\frac{1}{a}\int\frac{1}{\left(\frac{x}{a}\right)^2+1}\ \mathrm{d}\left(\frac{x}{a}\right)\\
     &=\frac{1}{a}\arctan\left(\frac{x}{a}\right)+C \tag*{$\blacksquare$}
\end{align*}
$$

</details>


### 20
$$
\int \frac{1}{\left(x^2+a^2\right)^n}\ \mathrm{d}x=\frac{x}{2\left(n-1\right)a^2\left(x^2+a^2\right)^{n-1}}+\frac{2n-3}{2\left(n-1\right)a^2}\int \frac{1}{\left(x^2+a^2\right)^{n-1}}\ \mathrm{d}x
$$
<br/>
<details>
<summary><span className="math-keyword">Derivation</span></summary>

$$
\begin{align*}
    I&=\int{\frac{1}{(x^2+a^2)^n}}\ \mathrm{d}x\\
     &=\frac{x}{(x^2+a^2)^n}-\int{x}\ \mathrm{d}\left(\frac{1}{(x^2+a^2)^n} \right)   &&\text{(Integration by Parts)}\\
     &=\frac{x}{(x^2+a^2)^n}-\int{x\cdot \frac{-n\cdot 2x}{(x^2+a^2)^{n+1}}}\ \mathrm{d}x\\ 
     &=\frac{x}{(x^2+a^2)^n}+2n\int{\frac{x^2}{(x^2+a^2)^{n+1}}}\ \mathrm{d}x\\ 
     &=\frac{x}{(x^2+a^2)^n}+2n\int{\frac{x^2+a^2-a^2}{(x^2+a^2)^{n+1}}}\ \mathrm{d}x\\ 
     &=\frac{x}{(x^2+a^2)^n}+2n\int{\frac{1}{(x^2+a^2)^{n}}}\ \mathrm{d}x-2na^2\int{\frac{1}{(x^2+a^2)^{n+1}}}\ \mathrm{d}x\\ 
\end{align*}
$$
$$
\begin{align*}
    \implies \int{\frac{1}{(x^2+a^2)^n}}\ \mathrm{d}x&= \frac{x}{(x^2+a^2)^n}+2n\int{\frac{1}{(x^2+a^2)^{n}}}\ \mathrm{d}x-2na^2\int{\frac{1}{(x^2+a^2)^{n+1}}}\ \mathrm{d}x\\
    2na^2\int{\frac{1}{(x^2+a^2)^{n+1}}}\ \mathrm{d}x&= \frac{x}{(x^2+a^2)^n}+(2n-1)\int{\frac{1}{(x^2+a^2)^{n}}}\ \mathrm{d}x
\end{align*}
$$
Replace $n+1$ to $n$ and $n$ to $n-1$:
$$
\begin{align*}
    2(n-1)a^2\int{\frac{1}{(x^2+a^2)^{n}}}\ \mathrm{d}x&= \frac{x}{(x^2+a^2)^{n-1}}+[2(n-1)-1]\int{\frac{1}{(x^2+a^2)^{n-1}}}\ \mathrm{d}x\\
    \int \frac{1}{\left(x^2+a^2\right)^n}\ \mathrm{d}x&=\frac{x}{2\left(n-1\right)a^2\left(x^2+a^2\right)^{n-1}}+\frac{2n-3}{2\left(n-1\right)a^2}\int \frac{1}{\left(x^2+a^2\right)^{n-1}}\ \mathrm{d}x  \tag*{$\blacksquare$}
\end{align*}
$$

</p>
</details>

### 21
$$
\int \frac{1}{x^2-a^2}\ \mathrm{d}x=\frac{1}{2a}\ln{\left| \frac{x-a}{x+a}\right|}+C
$$
<br/>
<details>
<summary><span className="math-keyword">Derivation</span></summary>

$$
\begin{align*}
    I&=\int{\frac{1}{x^2-a^2}}\ \mathrm{d}x\\
     &=\int{\frac{x+a-(x-a)}{2a(x^2-a^2)}}\ \mathrm{d}x\\
     &=\frac{1}{2a}\left[\int{\frac{(x+a)-(x-a)}{(x+a)(x-a)}}\ \mathrm{d}x \right]\\
     &=\frac{1}{2a}\left(\int{\frac{1}{x-a}}\ \mathrm{d}x-\int{\frac{1}{x+a}}\ \mathrm{d}x\right)\\
     &=\frac{1}{2a}\left(\ln{\left|x-a\right|}-\ln{\left|x+a\right|}+C\right)\\
     &=\frac{1}{2a}\ln{\left| \frac{x-a}{x+a}\right|}+C \tag*{$\blacksquare$}
\end{align*}
$$

</details>
