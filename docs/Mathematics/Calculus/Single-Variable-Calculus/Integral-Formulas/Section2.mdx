# Integral with $\sqrt{ax+b}$


### 10
$$
\int \sqrt{ax+b}\ \mathrm{d}x=\frac{2}{3a}\sqrt{(ax+b)^3}+C
$$
<br/>
<details>
<summary><span className="math-keyword">Derivation</span></summary>

Let $u=ax+b$, then $\mathrm{d}u=a\ \mathrm{d}x \implies \mathrm{d}x=\frac{\mathrm{d}u}{a}$

$$
\begin{align*}
    I&=\int{\sqrt{ax+b}}\ \mathrm{d}x\\
     &=\int{\frac{\sqrt{u}}{a}}\ \mathrm{d}u\\
     &=\frac{u^{\frac{1}{2}+1}}{a(\frac{1}{2}+1)}+C\\
     &=\frac{2\sqrt{u^3}}{3a}+C\\
     &=\frac{2}{3a}\sqrt{(ax+b)^3}+C \tag*{$\blacksquare$}
\end{align*}
$$
</details>

### 11
$$
\int x\sqrt {ax+b}\ \ \mathrm{d}x=\frac{2}{15a^2}(3ax-2b)\sqrt{(ax+b)^3}+C
$$
<br/>
<details>
<summary><span className="math-keyword">Derivation</span></summary>

Let $u=\sqrt{ax+b}$, then $x=\frac{u^2-b}{a}$, $\mathrm{d}u=\frac{a}{2\sqrt{ax+b}}\mathrm{d}x=\frac{a}{2u}{\mathrm{d}}x \implies \mathrm{d}x=\frac{2u}{a}{\mathrm{d}}u$

$$
\begin{align*}
    I&=\int{x\sqrt{ax+b}}\ \mathrm{d}x\\
     &=\int\frac{u^2-b}{a}\cdot u\cdot \frac{2u}{a}\ \mathrm{d}u &&\text{(Back Substitution)}\\
     &=\frac{2}{a^2}\int{(u^4-bu^2)}\ \mathrm{d}u\\
     &=\frac{2}{a^2}\left(\frac{1}{5}u^5-\frac{b}{3}u^3 +C\right)\\
     &=\frac{2}{a^2}\left(\frac{3u^5-5bu^3}{15}+C\right)\\
     &=\frac{2}{a^2}\left[\frac{(3u^2-5b)u^3}{15}+C\right]\\
     &=\frac{2}{15a^2}\left[3(\sqrt{ax+b})^2-5b\right]\sqrt{(ax+b)^3}+C &&(\text{plug in }u=\sqrt{ax+b})\\
     &=\frac{2}{15a^2}(3ax-2b)\sqrt{(ax+b)^3}+C \tag*{$\blacksquare$}
\end{align*}
$$

### 12
$$
\int x^2\sqrt {ax+b}\ \ \mathrm{d}x= \frac{2}{105a^3}(15a^2x^2-12abx+8b^2)\sqrt{(ax+b)^3}+C
$$
<br/>
<details>
<summary><span className="math-keyword">Derivation</span></summary>

Let $u=\sqrt{ax+b}$, then $x=\frac{u^2-b}{a}$, $\mathrm{d}u=\frac{a}{2\sqrt{ax+b}}\mathrm{d}x=\frac{a}{2u}{\mathrm{d}}x \implies \mathrm{d}x=\frac{2u}{a}{\mathrm{d}}u$
$$
\begin{align*}
    I&=\int{x^2\sqrt{ax+b}}\ \mathrm{d}x\\
     &=\int{\left(\frac{u^2-b}{a}\right)^2\cdot u\cdot \frac{2u}{a}}\ \mathrm{d}u &&\text{(Back Substitution)}\\
     &=\frac{2}{a^3}\int{(u^4-2bu^2+b^2)(u^2)}\ \mathrm{d}u\\
     &=\frac{2}{a^3}\int{(u^6-2bu^4+b^2u^2)}\ \mathrm{d}u\\
     &=\frac{2}{a^2}\left(\frac{1}{7}u^7-\frac{2b}{5}u^5+\frac{b^2}{3}u^3 +C\right)\\
     &=\frac{2}{a^2}\left(\frac{15u^7-42bu^5+35b^2u^3}{105}+C\right)\\
     &=\frac{2}{a^2}\left[\frac{(15u^4-42bu^2+35b^2)u^3}{105}+C\right]\\
     &=\frac{2}{105a^3}\left[15(\sqrt{ax+b})^4-42(\sqrt{ax+b})^2+35b^2\right]\sqrt{(ax+b)^3}+C &&(\text{plug in }u=\sqrt{ax+b})\\
     &=\frac{2}{105a^3}(15a^2x^2-12abx+8b^2)\sqrt{(ax+b)^3}+C \tag*{$\blacksquare$}
\end{align*}
$$

### 13
$$
\int \frac{x}{\sqrt{ax+b}}\ \mathrm{d}x=\frac{2}{3a^2}(ax-2b)\sqrt{ax+b}+C
$$
<br/>
<details>
<summary><span className="math-keyword">Derivation</span></summary>

Let $u=\sqrt{ax+b}$, then $x=\frac{u^2-b}{a}$, $\mathrm{d}u=\frac{a}{2\sqrt{ax+b}}\mathrm{d}x=\frac{a}{2u}{\mathrm{d}}x \implies \mathrm{d}x=\frac{2u}{a}{\mathrm{d}}u$
$$
\begin{align*}
    I&=\int{\frac{x}{\sqrt{ax+b}}}\ \mathrm{d}x\\
     &=\int{\frac{\frac{u^2-b}{a}}{u}}\frac{2u}{a}\ \mathrm{d}u &&\text{(Back Substitution)}\\
     &=\int{\frac{2(u^2-b)}{a^2}}\ \mathrm{d}u\\
     &=\frac{2}{a^2}\int{(u^2-b)}\ \mathrm{d}u\\
     &=\frac{2}{a^2}\left(\frac{1}{3}u^3-bu+C\right)\\
     &=\frac{2}{3a^2}\cdot (u^2-3b)\cdot u+C\\
     &=\frac{2}{3a^2}\cdot(ax+b-3b)\cdot \sqrt{ax+b}+C &&(\text{plug in }u=\sqrt{ax+b})\\
     &=\frac{2}{3a^2}(ax-2b)\sqrt{ax+b}+C \tag*{$\blacksquare$}
\end{align*}
$$

### 14
$$
\int\frac{x^2}{\sqrt{ax+b}}\ \mathrm{d}x=\frac{2}{15a^3}\left(3a^2x^2-4abx+8b^2\right)\sqrt{ax+b}+C
$$
<br/>
<details>
<summary><span className="math-keyword">Derivation</span></summary>

Let $u=\sqrt{ax+b}$, then $x=\frac{u^2-b}{a}$, $\mathrm{d}u=\frac{a}{2\sqrt{ax+b}}\mathrm{d}x=\frac{a}{2u}{\mathrm{d}}x \implies \mathrm{d}x=\frac{2u}{a}{\mathrm{d}}u$
$$
\begin{align*}
    I&=\int\frac{x^2}{\sqrt{ax+b}}\ \mathrm{d}x\\
     &=\int\frac{\left(\frac{u^2-b}{a} \right)^2}{u}\frac{2u}{a}\ \mathrm{d}u &&\text{(Back Substitution)}\\
     &=\frac{2}{a^3}\int{(u^2-b)^2}\ \mathrm{d}u\\
     &=\frac{2}{a^3}\int(u^4-2bu^2+b^2)\ \mathrm{d}u\\
     &=\frac{2}{a^3}\left(\frac{1}{5}u^5-\frac{2b}{3}u^3+b^2u+C\right)\\
     &=\frac{2}{a^3}\cdot\left(\frac{3u^4-10bu^2+15b^2}{15}\right)\cdot u+C\\
     &=\frac{2}{15a^3}\cdot \left[3(ax+b)^2-10b(ax+b)+15b^2\right]\cdot \sqrt{ax+b}+C &&(\text{plug in }u=\sqrt{ax+b})\\
     &=\frac{2}{15a^3}(3a^2x^2-4abx+8b^2)\sqrt{ax+b}+C \tag*{$\blacksquare$}
\end{align*}
$$

### 15
$$
\int \frac{1}{x \sqrt{ax+b}} \ \mathrm{d}x = 
\left\{ \begin{aligned}
&\frac{1}{\sqrt{b}} \ln{\left|\frac{\sqrt{ax+b}-\sqrt{b}}{\sqrt{ax+b}+\sqrt{b}}\right|} + C \ \left(b>0\right)\\
&\frac{2}{\sqrt{-b}} \arctan{\sqrt{\frac{ax+b}{-b}}}+C \ \left(b<0\right)
\end{aligned}
\right. 
$$
<br/>
<details>
<summary><span className="math-keyword">Derivation</span></summary>

Let $u=\sqrt{ax+b}$, then $x=\frac{u^2-b}{a}$, $\mathrm{d}u=\frac{a}{2\sqrt{ax+b}}\mathrm{d}x=\frac{a}{2u}{\mathrm{d}}x \implies \mathrm{d}x=\frac{2u}{a}{\mathrm{d}}u$
$$
\begin{align*}
    I&=\int{\frac{1}{x\sqrt{ax+b}}}\ \mathrm{d}x\\
     &=\int{\frac{1}{\frac{u^2-b}{a}\cdot u}}\cdot \frac{2u}{a}\ \mathrm{d}u &&\text{(Back Substitution)} \\
     &=2\int{\frac{1}{u^2-b}}\ \mathrm{d}u
\end{align*}
$$
When $b>0$ : 
$$
\begin{align*}
    2\int{\frac{1}{u^2-b}}\ \mathrm{d}u&=2\int{\frac{1}{u^2-(\sqrt{b})^2}}\ \mathrm{d}u\\
                             &=\frac{1}{\sqrt{b}}\ln{\left|\frac{u-\sqrt{b}}{u+\sqrt{b}}\right|}+C &&(\#21)\\
                             &=\frac{1}{\sqrt{b}}\ln{\left|\frac{\sqrt{ax+b}-\sqrt{b}}{\sqrt{ax+b}+\sqrt{b}}\right|}+C &&(\text{plug in }u=\sqrt{ax+b})
\end{align*}
$$
When $b<0$ : 
$$
\begin{align*}
    2\int{\frac{1}{u^2-b}}\ \mathrm{d}u&=2\int{\frac{1}{u^2+(\sqrt{-b})^2}}\ \mathrm{d}u\\
                                       &=2\left[\frac{1}{\sqrt{-b}}\arctan{\left(\frac{u}{\sqrt{-b}}\right)}+C\right] &&(\#19)\\
                                       &=\frac{2}{\sqrt{-b}}\arctan{\sqrt{\frac{ax+b}{-b}}}+C &&(\text{plug in }u=\sqrt{ax+b})
\end{align*}
$$
Hence: 
$$
\int \frac{1}{x \sqrt{ax+b}} \ \mathrm{d}x = 
\left\{ \begin{aligned}
&\frac{1}{\sqrt{b}} \ln{\left|\frac{\sqrt{ax+b}-\sqrt{b}}{\sqrt{ax+b}+\sqrt{b}}\right|} + C \ \left(b>0\right)\\
&\frac{2}{\sqrt{-b}} \arctan{\sqrt{\frac{ax+b}{-b}}}+C \ \left(b<0\right)
\end{aligned}
\right. 
\tag*{$\blacksquare$}
$$

### 16
$$
\int{\frac{\sqrt{ax+b}}{x^2}}\ \mathrm{d}x=-\frac{\sqrt{ax+b}}{x}+\frac{a}{2}\int{\frac{1}{x\sqrt{ax+b}}}\ \mathrm{d}x
$$
<br/>
<details>
<summary><span className="math-keyword">Derivation</span></summary>

$$
\begin{align*}
    I&=\int{\frac{\sqrt{ax+b}}{x^2}}\ \mathrm{d}x\\
     &=\int{\sqrt{ax+b}}\cdot \frac{1}{x^2}\ \mathrm{d}x\\ 
     &=\int{\sqrt{ax+b}}\ \mathrm{d}\left(-\frac{1}{x}\right)\\
     &=-\frac{\sqrt{ax+b}}{x}-\int{-\frac{1}{x}\ \mathrm{d}(\sqrt{ax+b})} &&(\text{Integral By Parts})\\
     &=-\frac{\sqrt{ax+b}}{x}+\frac{a}{2}\int{\frac{1}{x\sqrt{ax+b}}}\ \mathrm{d}x \tag*{$\blacksquare$}
\end{align*}
$$
