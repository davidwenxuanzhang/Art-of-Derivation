Trigonometry Identities

This page will include all common trigonometry identites that the author know; with Derivations. Those are really important, and the author highly recommende to memorize all of those.

Pythagorean Identities​

1​

sin⁡2x+cos⁡2x=1\sin^2{x}+\cos^2{x}=1sin2x+cos2x=1
Derivation

2​

cot⁡2x+1=csc⁡2x\cot^2{x}+1=\csc^2{x}cot2x+1=csc2x
Derivation

3​

tan⁡2x+1=sec⁡2x\tan^2{x}+1=\sec^2{x}tan2x+1=sec2x
Derivation

Angle Sum and Difference Identities​

4​

sin⁡(α+β)=sin⁡αcos⁡β+cos⁡αsin⁡β\sin{\left(\alpha+\beta\right)}=\sin{\alpha}\cos{\beta}+\cos{\alpha}\sin{\beta}sin(α+β)=sinαcosβ+cosαsinβ
Derivation
sin⁡β=BCAB=BC1  ⟹  BC=sin⁡βcos⁡β=ACAB=AC1  ⟹  AC=cos⁡βsin⁡α=CDAC=CDcos⁡β  ⟹  CD=sin⁡αcos⁡βcos⁡α=CFBC=CFsin⁡β  ⟹  CF=cos⁡αsin⁡β \begin{align*} \blue{\sin{\beta}}=\frac{BC}{AB}=\frac{BC}{1}\implies BC&=\blue{\sin{\beta}}\\ \blue{\cos{\beta}}=\frac{AC}{AB}=\frac{AC}{1}\implies AC&=\blue{\cos{\beta}}\\ \green{\sin{\alpha}}=\frac{CD}{AC}=\frac{CD}{\blue{\cos{\beta}}}\implies CD&=\green{\sin{\alpha}}\blue{\cos{\beta}}\\ \green{\cos{\alpha}}=\frac{CF}{BC}=\frac{CF}{\blue{\sin{\beta}}}\implies CF&=\green{\cos{\alpha}}\blue{\sin{\beta}}\\ \end{align*}sinβ=ABBC​=1BC​⟹BCcosβ=ABAC​=1AC​⟹ACsinα=ACCD​=cosβCD​⟹CDcosα=BCCF​=sinβCF​⟹CF​=sinβ=cosβ=sinαcosβ=cosαsinβ​sin⁡(α+β)=BEAB=BE1=CD+CF=sin⁡αcos⁡β+cos⁡αsin⁡β \begin{align*} \sin{(\green{\alpha}+\blue{\beta})}&=\frac{BE}{AB}\\ &=\frac{BE}{1}\\ &=CD+CF\\ &=\green{\sin{\alpha}}\blue{\cos{\beta}}+\green{\cos{\alpha}}\blue{\sin{\beta}} \tag*{$\blacksquare$} \end{align*}sin(α+β)​=ABBE​=1BE​=CD+CF=sinαcosβ+cosαsinβ​■​

5​

sin⁡(α−β)=sin⁡αcos⁡β−cos⁡αsin⁡β\sin{\left(\alpha-\beta\right)}=\sin{\alpha}\cos{\beta}-\cos{\alpha}\sin{\beta}sin(α−β)=sinαcosβ−cosαsinβ
Derivation

6​

cos⁡(α+β)=cos⁡αcos⁡β−sin⁡αsin⁡β\cos{\left(\alpha+\beta\right)}=\cos{\alpha}\cos{\beta}-\sin{\alpha}\sin{\beta}cos(α+β)=cosαcosβ−sinαsinβ
Derivation

7​

cos⁡(α−β)=cos⁡αcos⁡β+sin⁡αsin⁡β\cos{\left(\alpha-\beta\right)}=\cos{\alpha}\cos{\beta}+\sin{\alpha}\sin{\beta}cos(α−β)=cosαcosβ+sinαsinβ
Derivation

8​

tan⁡(α+β)=tan⁡α+tan⁡β1−tan⁡αtan⁡β\tan{\left(\alpha+\beta\right)}=\frac{\tan{\alpha}+\tan{\beta}}{1-\tan{\alpha}\tan{\beta}}tan(α+β)=1−tanαtanβtanα+tanβ​
Derivation

9​

tan⁡(α−β)=tan⁡α−tan⁡β1+tan⁡αtan⁡β\tan{\left(\alpha-\beta\right)}=\frac{\tan{\alpha}-\tan{\beta}}{1+\tan{\alpha}\tan{\beta}}tan(α−β)=1+tanαtanβtanα−tanβ​
Derivation

Multiple Angle Formulas​

10​

sin⁡2α=2sin⁡αcos⁡α\sin{2\alpha}=2\sin{\alpha}\cos{\alpha}sin2α=2sinαcosα
Derivation
sin⁡2α=sin⁡(α+α)=sin⁡αcos⁡α+cos⁡αsin⁡α(Trig Identity #4)=2sin⁡αcos⁡α\begin{align*} \sin{2\alpha}&=\sin{(\red{\alpha}+\blue{\alpha})}\\ &=\sin{\red{\alpha}}\cos{\blue{\alpha}}+\cos{\red{\alpha}}\sin{\blue{\alpha}} &&({\text{Trig Identity \#4}})\\ &=2\sin{\alpha}\cos{\alpha} \tag*{$\blacksquare$} \end{align*}sin2α​=sin(α+α)=sinαcosα+cosαsinα=2sinαcosα​​(Trig Identity #4)​■​

Trig Identity #4

11​

cos⁡2α=cos⁡2α−sin⁡2α=2cos⁡2α−1=1−2sin⁡2α\cos{2\alpha}=\cos^2{\alpha}-\sin^2{\alpha}=2\cos^2{\alpha}-1=1-2\sin^2{\alpha}cos2α=cos2α−sin2α=2cos2α−1=1−2sin2α
Derivation
cos⁡2α=cos⁡(α+α)=cos⁡αcos⁡α−sin⁡αsin⁡α(Trig Identity #6)=cos⁡2α−sin⁡2α\begin{align*} \cos{2\alpha}&=\cos{(\red{\alpha}+\blue{\alpha})}\\ &=\cos{\red{\alpha}}\cos{\blue{\alpha}}-\sin{\red{\alpha}}\sin{\blue{\alpha}} &&({\text{Trig Identity \#6}})\\ &=\cos^2{\alpha}-\sin^2{\alpha}\\ & \tag*{$\blacksquare$} \end{align*}cos2α​=cos(α+α)=cosαcosα−sinαsinα=cos2α−sin2α​(Trig Identity #6)■​

Trig Identity #6

12​

sin⁡3α=−4sin⁡3α+3sin⁡α\sin{3\alpha}=-4\sin^3{\alpha}+3\sin{\alpha}sin3α=−4sin3α+3sinα
Derivation

13​

cos⁡3α=4cos⁡3α−3cos⁡α\cos{3\alpha}=4\cos^3{\alpha}-3\cos{\alpha}cos3α=4cos3α−3cosα
Derivation

Power-Reduction Formulas​

14​

sin⁡2α=1−cos⁡2α2\sin^2{\alpha}=\frac{1-\cos{2\alpha}}{2}sin2α=21−cos2α​
Derivation

15​

cos⁡2α=1+cos⁡2α2\cos^2{\alpha}=\frac{1+\cos{2\alpha}}{2}cos2α=21+cos2α​
Derivation

Half Angle Formulas​

Why ±\pm± ?

16​

sin⁡12α=±1−cos⁡α2\sin{\frac{1}{2}\alpha}=\pm \sqrt{\frac{1-\cos{\alpha}}{2}}sin21​α=±21−cosα​​
Derivation

17​

cos⁡12α=±1+cos⁡α2\cos{\frac{1}{2}\alpha}=\pm \sqrt{\frac{1+\cos{\alpha}}{2}}cos21​α=±21+cosα​​
Derivation

18​

tan⁡12α=±1−cos⁡α1+cos⁡α=1−cos⁡αsin⁡α=sin⁡α1+cos⁡α\tan{\frac{1}{2}\alpha}=\pm \sqrt{\frac{1-\cos{\alpha}}{1+\cos{\alpha}}}=\frac{1-\cos{\alpha}}{\sin{\alpha}}=\frac{\sin{\alpha}}{1+\cos{\alpha}}tan21​α=±1+cosα1−cosα​​=sinα1−cosα​=1+cosαsinα​
