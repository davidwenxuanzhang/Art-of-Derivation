## Angle Sum and Difference Identities

### 4
$$
\sin{\left(\alpha+\beta\right)}=\sin{\alpha}\cos{\beta}+\cos{\alpha}\sin{\beta}
$$
<br/>
<details>
<summary><span className="math-keyword">Derivation</span></summary>

<div className="deriv2col">

  <div>
    <img src={useBaseUrl('/img/sines_sum.png')} width="400" />
  </div>

  <div>
    $$
        \begin{align*}
            \blue{\sin{\beta}}=\frac{BC}{AB}=\frac{BC}{1}\implies BC&=\blue{\sin{\beta}}\\
            \blue{\cos{\beta}}=\frac{AC}{AB}=\frac{AC}{1}\implies AC&=\blue{\cos{\beta}}\\
            \green{\sin{\alpha}}=\frac{CD}{AC}=\frac{CD}{\blue{\cos{\beta}}}\implies CD&=\green{\sin{\alpha}}\blue{\cos{\beta}}\\
            \green{\cos{\alpha}}=\frac{CF}{BC}=\frac{CF}{\blue{\sin{\beta}}}\implies CF&=\green{\cos{\alpha}}\blue{\sin{\beta}}\\
        \end{align*}
    $$
    $$
        \begin{align*}
                \sin{(\green{\alpha}+\blue{\beta})}&=\frac{BE}{AB}=\frac{BE}{1}=DF\\
                                                   &=CD+CF\\
                                                   &=\green{\sin{\alpha}}\blue{\cos{\beta}}+\green{\cos{\alpha}}\blue{\sin{\beta}} \tag*{$\blacksquare$}
        \end{align*}
    $$
  </div>

</div>

</details>

### 6
$$
\cos{\left(\alpha+\beta\right)}=\cos{\alpha}\cos{\beta}-\sin{\alpha}\sin{\beta}
$$
<br/>
<details>
<summary><span className="math-keyword">Derivation</span></summary>

<div className="deriv2col">

  <div>
    <img src={useBaseUrl('/img/Cosines-Sum.png')} width="375" />
  </div>

  <div>
    $$
        \begin{align*}
            \blue{\sin{\beta}}=\frac{BC}{AB}=\frac{BC}{1}\implies BC&=\blue{\sin{\beta}}\\
            \blue{\cos{\beta}}=\frac{AC}{AB}=\frac{AC}{1}\implies AC&=\blue{\cos{\beta}}\\
            \green{\sin{\alpha}}=\frac{BF}{AC}=\frac{BF}{\blue{\sin{\beta}}}\implies BF&=\green{\sin{\alpha}}\blue{\sin{\beta}}\\
            \green{\cos{\alpha}}=\frac{AD}{BC}=\frac{AD}{\blue{\cos{\beta}}}\implies AD&=\green{\cos{\alpha}}\blue{\cos{\beta}}\\
        \end{align*}
    $$
    $$
        \begin{align*}
                \cos{(\green{\alpha}+\blue{\beta})}&=\frac{AE}{AB}=\frac{AE}{1}\\
                                                   &=AD-ED\\
                                                   &=AD-BF\\
                                                   &=\green{\cos{\alpha}}\blue{\cos{\beta}}-\green{\sin{\alpha}}\blue{\sin{\beta}} \tag*{$\blacksquare$}
        \end{align*}
    $$
  </div>

</div>

</details>

## Multiple Angle Formulas

### 10
$$
\sin{2\alpha}=2\sin{\alpha}\cos{\alpha}
$$
<br/>
<details>
<summary><span className="math-keyword">Derivation</span></summary>

$$
\begin{align*}
    \sin{2\alpha}&=\sin{(\red{\alpha}+\blue{\alpha})}\\
                 &=\sin{\red{\alpha}}\cos{\blue{\alpha}}+\cos{\red{\alpha}}\sin{\blue{\alpha}} &&({\text{Trig Identity \#4}})\\
                 &=2\sin{\alpha}\cos{\alpha} \tag*{$\blacksquare$}
\end{align*}
$$

[Trig Identity \#4](docs/Mathematics/Trigonometry/Trigonometry-Identities.mdx#4)

</details>


### 11
$$
\cos{2\alpha}=\cos^2{\alpha}-\sin^2{\alpha}=2\cos^2{\alpha}-1=1-2\sin^2{\alpha}
$$
<br/>
<details>
<summary><span className="math-keyword">Derivation</span></summary>

$$
\begin{align*}
    \cos{2\alpha}&=\cos{(\red{\alpha}+\blue{\alpha})}\\
                 &=\cos{\red{\alpha}}\cos{\blue{\alpha}}-\sin{\red{\alpha}}\sin{\blue{\alpha}} &&({\text{Trig Identity \#6}})\\
                 &=\cos^2{\alpha}-\sin^2{\alpha}\\
                 & \tag*{$\blacksquare$}
\end{align*}
$$
