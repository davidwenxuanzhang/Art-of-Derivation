## Angle Sum and Difference Identities

### 4
$$
\sin{\left(\alpha+\beta\right)}=\sin{\alpha}\cos{\beta}+\cos{\alpha}\sin{\beta}
$$
<br/>
<details>
<summary><span className="math-keyword">Derivation</span></summary>

<div className="deriv2col">

  <div>
    <img src={useBaseUrl('/img/sines_sum.png')} width="375" />
  </div>

  <div>
    $$
        \begin{align*}
            \blue{\sin{\beta}}=\frac{BC}{AB}=\frac{BC}{1}\implies BC&=\blue{\sin{\beta}}\\
            \blue{\cos{\beta}}=\frac{AC}{AB}=\frac{AC}{1}\implies AC&=\blue{\cos{\beta}}\\
            \green{\sin{\alpha}}=\frac{CD}{AC}=\frac{CD}{\blue{\cos{\beta}}}\implies CD&=\green{\sin{\alpha}}\blue{\cos{\beta}}\\
            \green{\cos{\alpha}}=\frac{CF}{BC}=\frac{CF}{\blue{\sin{\beta}}}\implies CF&=\green{\cos{\alpha}}\blue{\sin{\beta}}\\
        \end{align*}
    $$
    $$
        \begin{align*}
                \sin{(\green{\alpha}+\blue{\beta})}&=\frac{BE}{AB}=\frac{BE}{1}=DF\\
                                                   &=CD+CF\\
                                                   &=\green{\sin{\alpha}}\blue{\cos{\beta}}+\green{\cos{\alpha}}\blue{\sin{\beta}} \tag*{$\blacksquare$}
        \end{align*}
    $$
  </div>

</div>

</details>

### 5
$$
\sin{\left(\alpha-\beta\right)}=\sin{\alpha}\cos{\beta}-\cos{\alpha}\sin{\beta}
$$
<br/>
<details>
<summary><span className="math-keyword">Derivation</span></summary>

<div className="deriv2col">

  <div>
    <img src={useBaseUrl('/img/Sines-Diff.png')} width="375" />
  </div>

  <div>
    $$
        \begin{align*}
            \blue{\sin{\beta}}=\frac{BC}{AB}=\frac{BC}{1}\implies BC&=\blue{\sin{\beta}}\\
            \blue{\cos{\beta}}=\frac{AC}{AB}=\frac{AC}{1}\implies AC&=\blue{\cos{\beta}}\\
            \green{\sin{\alpha}}=\frac{AD}{AC}=\frac{AD}{\blue{\cos{\beta}}}\implies AD&=\green{\sin{\alpha}}\blue{\cos{\beta}}\\
            \green{\cos{\alpha}}=\frac{BF}{BC}=\frac{BF}{\blue{\sin{\beta}}}\implies BF&=\green{\cos{\alpha}}\blue{\sin{\beta}}\\
        \end{align*}
    $$
    $$
        \begin{align*}
                \sin{(\green{\alpha}-\blue{\beta})}&=\frac{BG}{AB}=\frac{BG}{1}=AE\\
                                                   &=AD-ED\\
                                                   &=AD-BF\\
                                                   &=\green{\sin{\alpha}}\blue{\cos{\beta}}-\green{\cos{\alpha}}\blue{\sin{\beta}} \tag*{$\blacksquare$}

### 8
$$
\tan{\left(\alpha+\beta\right)}=\frac{\tan{\alpha}+\tan{\beta}}{1-\tan{\alpha}\tan{\beta}}
$$
<br/>
<details>
<summary><span className="math-keyword">Derivation</span></summary>

<div className="deriv2col">

  <div>
    <img src={useBaseUrl('/img/Tangents-Sum.png')} width="375" />
  </div>

  <div>
    $$
        \begin{align*}
            \green{\tan{\alpha}}=\frac{CD}{AD}=\frac{CD}{1}\implies CD&=\green{\tan{\alpha}}\\
            \green{\sec{\alpha}}=\frac{AC}{AD}=\frac{AC}{1}\implies AC&=\green{\sec{\alpha}}\\
            \blue{\tan{\beta}}=\frac{BC}{AC}=\frac{BC}{\green{\sec{\alpha}}}\implies BC&=\green{\sec{\alpha}}\blue{\tan{\beta}}\\
            \green{\sec{\alpha}}=\frac{BC}{CF}=\frac{\green{\sec{\alpha}}\blue{\tan{\beta}}}{CF}\implies CF&=\blue{\tan{\beta}}\\
            \green{\tan{\alpha}}=\frac{BF}{CF}=\frac{BF}{\blue{\tan{\beta}}}\implies BF&=\green{\tan{\alpha}}\blue{\tan{\beta}}
        \end{align*}
    $$
    $$
        \begin{align*}
                \tan{(\green{\alpha}+\blue{\beta})}&=\frac{BE}{AE}=\frac{DF}{AE}=\frac{CD+CF}{AD-BF}\\
                                                   &=\frac{\green{\tan{\alpha}}+\blue{\tan{\beta}}}{1-\green{\tan{\alpha}}\blue{\tan{\beta}}} \tag*{$\blacksquare$}
        \end{align*}
    $$
  </div>

</div>

</details>

## Multiple Angle Formulas

### 10
$$
\sin{2\alpha}=2\sin{\alpha}\cos{\alpha}
$$
<br/>
<details>
<summary><span className="math-keyword">Derivation</span></summary>

$$
\begin{align*}
    \sin{2\alpha}&=\sin{({\alpha}+{\alpha})}\\
                 &=\sin{{\alpha}}\cos{{\alpha}}+\cos{{\alpha}}\sin{{\alpha}} &&({\text{Trig Identity \#4}})\\
                 &=2\sin{\alpha}\cos{\alpha} \tag*{$\blacksquare$}
\end{align*}
$$

### 11
$$
\cos{2\alpha}=\cos^2{\alpha}-\sin^2{\alpha}=2\cos^2{\alpha}-1=1-2\sin^2{\alpha}
$$
<br/>
<details>
<summary><span className="math-keyword">Derivation</span></summary>

$$
\begin{align*}
    \cos{2\alpha}&=\cos{({\alpha}+{\alpha})}\\
                 &=\cos{{\alpha}}\cos{{\alpha}}-\sin{{\alpha}}\sin{{\alpha}} &&({\text{Trig Identity \#6}})\\
                 &=\cos^2{\alpha}-\sin^2{\alpha}\\
                 &=\cos^2{\alpha}-(1-\cos^2{\alpha})=2\cos^2{\alpha}-1 &&({\text{Trig Identity \#1}})\\
                 &=(1-\sin^2{\alpha})-\sin^2{\alpha}=1-2\sin^2{\alpha}&&({\text{Trig Identity \#1}})  \tag*{$\blacksquare$}
\end{align*}
$$

### 12
$$
\sin{3\alpha}=-4\sin^3{\alpha}+3\sin{\alpha}
$$
<br/>
<details>
<summary><span className="math-keyword">Derivation</span></summary>

$$
\begin{align*}
    \sin{3\alpha}&=\sin{({2\alpha}+{\alpha})}\\
                 &=\sin{{2\alpha}}\cos{{\alpha}}+\cos{{2\alpha}}\sin{{\alpha}} &&({\text{Trig Identity \#4}})\\
                 &=2\sin{\alpha}\cos{\alpha}\cos{\alpha}+\cos{{2\alpha}}\sin{{\alpha}} &&({\text{Trig Identity \#10}})\\
                 &=2\sin{\alpha}(\cos^2{\alpha})+(1-2\sin^2{\alpha})\sin{{\alpha}}  &&({\text{Trig Identity \#11}})\\
                 &=2\sin{\alpha}(1-\sin^2{\alpha})+(1-2\sin^2{\alpha})\sin{{\alpha}}  &&({\text{Trig Identity \#1}})\\
                 &=2\sin{\alpha}-2\sin^3{\alpha}+\sin{\alpha}-2\sin^3{\alpha}\\
                 &=-4\sin^3{\alpha}+3\sin{\alpha} \tag*{$\blacksquare$}
\end{align*}
$$

[Trig Identity \#4](docs/Mathematics/Trigonometry/Trigonometry-Identities.mdx#4)  $\quad$ [Trig Identity \#10](docs/Mathematics/Trigonometry/Trigonometry-Identities.mdx#10)

[Trig Identity \#11](docs/Mathematics/Trigonometry/Trigonometry-Identities.mdx#11) $\quad$  [Trig Identity \#1](docs/Mathematics/Trigonometry/Trigonometry-Identities.mdx#1)

</details>

### 13
$$
\cos{3\alpha}=4\cos^3{\alpha}-3\cos{\alpha}
$$
<br/>
<details>
<summary><span className="math-keyword">Derivation</span></summary>

$$
\begin{align*}
    \cos{3\alpha}&=\cos{({2\alpha}+{\alpha})}\\
                 &=\cos{{2\alpha}}\cos{{\alpha}}-\sin{{2\alpha}}\sin{{\alpha}} &&({\text{Trig Identity \#6}})\\
                 &=(2\cos^2{\alpha}-1)\cos{\alpha}-\sin{{2\alpha}}\sin{{\alpha}} &&({\text{Trig Identity \#11}})\\
                 &=2\cos^3{\alpha}-\cos{\alpha}-2\sin{\alpha}\cos{\alpha}\sin{{\alpha}}  &&({\text{Trig Identity \#10}})\\
                 &=2\cos^3{\alpha}-\cos{\alpha}-2\cos{\alpha}(1-\cos^2{\alpha})  &&({\text{Trig Identity \#1}})\\
                 &=2\cos^3{\alpha}-\cos{\alpha}-2\cos{\alpha}+2\cos^2{\alpha}\\
                 &=4\cos^3{\alpha}-3\cos{\alpha} \tag*{$\blacksquare$}
\end{align*}
$$
