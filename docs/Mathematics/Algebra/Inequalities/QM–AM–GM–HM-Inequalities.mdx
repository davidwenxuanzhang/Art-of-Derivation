:::tip QM–AM–GM–HM Inequalities

Let $x_1,x_2,\cdots, x_n$ are positive integers, then QM–AM–GM–HM Inequality states:

$$
    0<\underbrace{\frac{n}{\frac{1}{x_1}+\frac{1}{x_2}+\cdots+\frac{1}{x_n}}}_{\text{Harmonic Mean}}\leq \underbrace{\sqrt[n]{x_1x_2\cdots x_n}}_{\text{Geometric Mean}}\leq \underbrace{\frac{x_1+x_2+\cdots+x_n}{n}}_{\text{Arithmetic Mean}}\leq \underbrace{\sqrt{\frac{x_1^2+x_2^2+\cdots +x_n^2}{n}}}_{\text{Quadratic Mean}}
$$

:::
